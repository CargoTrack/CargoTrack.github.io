COMPANY

Get To Know Us

Global Delivery is a supply chain management company, a Freight forwarding expert, specializes in the provision of total logistic Services which Include specifically: Air Cargo, Sea or Ocean shipping, Express Parcel, Import Documentation, Customs Clearing, Consolidation, door delivery from and to 80 countries. We offer professional warehousing.

Delivery History

Global Delivery Services has been delivering Cargoes to and from around the world since 2011 and has thus acquired an extensive expertise in Freight Forwarding, Cargo handling and servicing, International trade representation, Procurement advisory and support, warehousing, Logistics and shipping agency.

Having based ourselves on the features demanded by large companies, we have created seamless process that are now working for busy executives, project managers, and companies in need of freight transportation from over 80 cities across Africa, Asia, USA, Europe and Middle East…

Government Approved

Global Delivery Services is a multi service company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of the United Kingdom of European and duly licensed under the UK companies and allied Matters Act 1990.

Global Delivery Services started as partnership ventures “Fedex”.

Our Vision

To be the most Capable and most responsive provider of Quality Logistics Services.

Our Mission

Ownership And Establishment

Global Delivery Services comprises of young and dynamic group of individual entrepreneurs and managers, rich in resources and with unique combination of skills.

Headed by, Patrick Haul, who carries with him many years of Business management and freight forwarding expertise. Global Delivery services holds the required statutory approval of the relevant bodies and regulatory authorities.

Global Delivery services was established to address the challenges of this millennium; the overwhelming yearning for quality delivery by consumers, including adequate methodology and experience which guarantee service that is equal to the task of meeting customers’ unique need for satisfaction.

We place an emphasis on excellence and customer service with flexible tailored solutions to meet customer’s needs. Our commitment to Service excellence is en-grained in the heart and minds of team members.
